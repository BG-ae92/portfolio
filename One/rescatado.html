rescatado

It's similar to breathing-
but not right.

Similar to closing your eyes-
for good.

A chance that well,
this could've been it.

At that moment
everything around
began to flutter.

The air spoke in light blue tones all around
as the white of the sky took over.

A light shined through
the color orange so sweet, warm.

And in that moment I felt that if left to fate
things would fall into place.

A net would catch
to take us away.

We'd end up somwhere
where darkness never falls
